Stay Safe Online – Checklist for Students

- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone, even if they sound official
- Always check carefully before accepting any “collect request” on UPI apps
- Avoid clicking on unknown links sent through SMS, WhatsApp, or email
- Use strong passwords and avoid using the same password everywhere
- Turn on two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Do not trust calls claiming to be from banks or delivery services without verification
- Keep your apps and phone software updated regularly
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for payments or sensitive activities

Financial / UPI Safety:
- Remember: you do NOT need to enter a PIN to receive money
- Double-check the receiver’s name before sending any payment

Reporting:
If you face any cyber fraud, report it immediately at:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
