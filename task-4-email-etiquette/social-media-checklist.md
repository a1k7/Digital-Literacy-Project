Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students

Do’s:
- Think before posting anything online
- Keep your profile clean and professional
- Share useful content like projects or achievements
- Be respectful while commenting or messaging others
- Use social media to learn and connect with people

Don’ts:
- Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Don’t engage in unnecessary arguments online
- Don’t copy others’ content without giving credit
- Don’t waste too much time scrolling without purpose
